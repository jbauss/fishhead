FishHead's Site

Moin! This website is my attempt at recapturing the spirit of the internet that initially made me fall in love with computers and technology: No platforms, no marketplaces, no engagement and no Algorithm. Just people carving out their own little spaces in the world wide web.

I intend this place to be a little window through which I can share ideas and creative output on my own terms, displayed in an uninvasive and distraction free environment.
