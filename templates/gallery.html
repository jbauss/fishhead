Oracle card designs

01.09.2024

I've become quite interested in Tarot and Oracle card designs ever since I first stumbled upon Yoshi Yoshitanis Tarot of the Divine, which replaces the classic tarot designs with illustrations of deities, folklore and fairy tales from all over the world.

After designing that Signalis postcard and getting a glimpse into the world of vector graphic design I played with the thought of designing some simple, stylized Oracle cards myself.

During the design process some recent video game ideas of mine started mixing with the card ideas and I'm currently working out how to combine the two into a whole!
